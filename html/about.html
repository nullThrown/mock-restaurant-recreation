All of us who live in the San Francisco Bay Area are also considered BAIANOS, like the BAIANOS who are from Bahia in Brazil. From Bahia to the Bay Bruno Matos has worked hard to become the successful business owner that he is. We are proud to give you our signature napoletana style pizzas inspired by the small towns in Italy, small bites that bring you a flavor of Brazil, with the attitude and personality of our local BAIANOS in San Francisco. With all three elements brought together in one, we present you BAIANO PIZZERIA.
